Privacy Policy

Welcome to NorWave. Your privacy is critically important to us. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

When you visit NorWave, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Information you provide, such as your name, email address, or account details when you register or contact us.
  • Usage Data: Non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior to help us improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To personalise your experience
  • To respond to your inquiries or requests
  • To analyse website performance and usage

Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners and service providers who assist us in operating our website or delivering our services, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow us to recognise your preferences and improve the functionality of our website. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the website may not function as intended.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal information we have collected about you
  • Request corrections to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing where applicable

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@norwave.com.

Thank you for trusting NorWave. Your privacy matters to us.
